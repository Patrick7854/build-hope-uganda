Our Programs

Five integrated thematic areas helping vulnerable refugees and host communities move from dependency toward self-reliance and resilience.

Program 01

Livelihoods

Supporting households to build sustainable income through vocational skills, climate-smart agriculture, financial literacy, VSLA, and market-oriented production.

Climate-Smart Agriculture

Promoting sustainable land use, agroforestry, and improved production for food security.

Vocational Skills

Training youth and adults in practical income skills including sandal making and other marketable crafts.

VSLA Methodology

Helping groups save, access small loans, and sustain household value chains.

Farming as a Business

Strengthening business planning, financial literacy, contract farming, and market linkages.

Farmer Field Schools

Supporting farmers to identify problems and make decisions in their own gardens.

Value Chain Support

Improving production, storage, market access, and household resilience.

Program 02

Energy & Environment

Advancing climate action through environmental conservation, tree growing, wetland protection, and community awareness on responsible natural resource use.

Tree Seedlings

Supporting households and groups with wood and fruit trees for restoration and nutrition.

Wetland Protection

Promoting behavioural change around farming practices and protection of fragile ecosystems.

Energy Awareness

Reducing risks on the amount of fire wood consumed by use of rocket lorena stoves and awareness creation on environmental protection.

Program 03

WASH

Improving sanitation, hygiene, and safe water practices through community-led behavioural change and role model households.

Sanitation Campaigns

Promoting latrine construction, use, and household sanitation in the targetted communities.

Hand Hygiene

Awareness sessions on handwashing and hygiene practices that reduce WASH-related diseases.

Role Model Households

Using local examples to encourage practical sanitation improvements across the settlement.

Program 04

Education

Promoting functional adult literacy and vocational learning for refugees whose formal education was disrupted by displacement.

Functional Adult Literacy

Helping adults build reading, writing, English, and practical communication skills.

Vocational Learning

Complementing formal education with hands-on skills that support income generation.

Mindset Change

Supporting learners to participate confidently in household, community, and economic life.

Program 05

GBV & Community-Based Protection

Reducing gender-based violence risks through community sensitization, survivor referral, social cohesion, and rights-based protection.

GBV Awareness

Community campaigns on prevention, reporting, and available support pathways.

Risk Mitigation

Addressing risks linked to poverty, firewood collection, water access, and isolated spaces.

Referral Support

Connecting survivors and vulnerable households to relevant service providers.

Program Strategy

01

02
