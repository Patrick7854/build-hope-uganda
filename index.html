Who We Are

Refugee-Led,
Community Driven

BUILD HOPE UGANDA was formalized by passionate refugee youth on 16 October 2020 after recognizing critical needs among women, men, youth, widows, child mothers, orphans, persons with disabilities, and child-headed households.
