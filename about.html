About BUILD HOPE UGANDA

A refugee-led humanitarian and development organization transforming communities to self-reliance and resilience.

Our Story

Born From Local Needs,
Led by Refugee Youth

BUILD HOPE UGANDA (BHU) is a refugee-led organization and national humanitarian and development agency working to reduce vulnerability by helping people realize their potential, build on their own assets, and respond to their own problems and needs.

BHU was formalized by passionate refugee youth on 16th October 2020 after seeing the critical needs of vulnerable women and men, youth, widows, child mothers, orphans, persons with disabilities, and child-headed households in Kyangwali Refugee Settlement and neighboring host communities.

The organization works in Kyangwali Refugee Settlement, Kyangwali Sub-County, Kikuube District, South Western Uganda, using integrated humanitarian and development programming to promote self-reliant and resilient households.

Vision, Mission & Values

👁

Vision

To ensure the most vulnerable refugee communities are supported with employable skills while fostering economic empowerment, social integration, community-based protection, and climate action techniques.

🎯

To empower the most vulnerable refugee communities to attain self-reliance and resilience through durable solutions.
